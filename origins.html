The ancient period in the Land of Israel includes the prehistoric periods, the Bronze Age, the Iron Age, the Persian period, the Hellenistic period, the Hasmonean period, and the Roman period. The Bronze Age is the period in which urbanization began in the Land of Israel and the establishment of fortified cities. The Iron Age saw the existence of the kingdoms of Israel and Judah. The Hellenistic period began with the conquests of Alexander the Great and Greek culture influenced the land. The Roman period began with the conquest of the land by Pompey and was characterized by Jewish resistance to Roman rule.
